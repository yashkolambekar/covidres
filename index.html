Donate for Elders

HelpAge India - Covid 19

Elders are at high risk and more vulnerable in COVID 19 more so if they are 80 years or above. Many Elders are struggling to survive. Many have lost their small income, many more are living with families whose income has stopped. Your contribution can help an elder survive and bring hope in this uncertain time.

Donate for Health Workers

Give India - #IndiaFightsCorona

Your contribution will help hospitals tackle the situation and protect their healthcare workers as they treat Covid-infected patients. You can help provide N95 Masks, PPE Kits, 3ply masks, bedsheet, hand sanitiser (1 litre) and refill oxygen cylinders at Covid care centres.
